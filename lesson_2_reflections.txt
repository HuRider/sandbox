Reflect: Merging Two Branches
Q: What is the result of merging two brances together? Why do we represent it in the diagram the way we do?
A: The result of merging two branches together combines all of the commits of the two branches.  Commits are combined and logged chronologically.  The automatic merging uses recursive logic that compares the common parent with the two distinct files.
